What is the purpose of Quick Sparks?

The purpose of Quick Sparks is to enable students to Rediscover the Ease & Joy of Learning and to Express the Genius within them using Brain Friendly Learning methods.

What is Quick Sparks about?

Quick sparks is about using the methodology of Multiple Intelligences in Children : Making them Word Smart, Logic Smart, Picture Smart, Body smart, Music Smart, People Smart and Self Smart to build a well rounded personality for themselves. Quick sparks programs are designed with exercises, techniques and activities for Whole Brain Learning : using both their Creative and Logical faculties together for more Effective Learning. Activities from Educational Kinesiology and games designed around proven memory techniques make academic learning more exciting than ever. Learning strategies and principles from the methodologies of Neuro Linguistic Programming (NLP) and Accelerated Learning are also incorporated. The role of music, emotions and movement in learning are explored. Children also participate in a variety of activities which support them in exploring Art, Music and Drama.

Quick Sparks Junior Camp

Age: 9 to 12
Duration: 3 days

What will your Child learn?

  • Success Strategies and Attitudes of Geniuses: How to approach Challenges and New Learning so as to make them Fun and enjoyable. Students also learn the attitudes and strategies to enhance their creativity.
  • Different Learning modes: Students enhance their flexibility in adapting to different modes of learning.
  • Exercises for Whole Brain Learning: Brain Gym Exercises to train their creative and logical faculties to work together in a balanced and integrated manner.
  • How to manage their mental-emotional states for effective learning and performance: Students learn to use their thinking in more directed and productive ways, hence improving their performance.
  • How memory works and How to develop a Super Memory - Memory Systems: Students are trained to make Learning and remembering Entertaining and easy using proven techniques, activities and games.
  • Visual Strategies for Spelling and Improved Vocabulary: Students learn simple and very effective learning strategies to enhance their repertoire when it comes to spellings and Vocabulary in languages.
  • How to learn mathematics easily: Proven techniques for
    • learning addition, subtraction, multiplication and division quickly and effectively
    • learning problem solving strategies in maths
    • learning math terminology
  • Communication & Leadership Skills: Students take part in activities and games that nurture their interpersonal skills and leadership potential.
